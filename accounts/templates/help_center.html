Dreamland Travel Care

Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and get assistance with your travel plans.

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a trip?

You can book by browsing packages, selecting your destination, and completing the booking form with traveler and payment details.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes. Cancellation and reschedule options depend on package terms, travel date, and partner policy.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept secure online payments, cards, UPI, and supported payment gateways for eligible bookings.

Travel Documents

Do I need a visa?

Visa requirements depend on destination. For international trips this is usually required. Please check package details.

What ID is required for domestic travel?

A valid government ID is required for domestic travel. Airlines and hotels may ask for matching booking details.

How do I upload my documents?

Share documents through the booking confirmation flow or contact support for assistance.

Packages & Offers

What's included in a tour package?

Packages typically include stay, sightseeing, selected meals, transport, and guided assistance based on itinerary.

Are there seasonal discounts?

Yes, seasonal and festive offers are available on selected routes and departure windows.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Yes, customization is available for many plans based on budget, days, and destination preferences.

Customer Support

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team via phone, email, or WhatsApp. Use the Need More Help section below.

What are your service hours?

Support is available Monday to Saturday during working hours. Exact timings may vary on holidays.

Is live chat available?

Live chat availability depends on support load. You can still reach us quickly through WhatsApp and phone support.
