Refund Policy

Please review this policy before confirming a booking. It explains when refunds apply and how they are processed.

Our Commitment

At Dream Trip Destinations, we follow a transparent refund workflow. All refund requests are reviewed against booking date, travel date, supplier terms, and services already consumed.

01

Cancellation Request

Refund processing starts only after a written cancellation request is received through registered email or support channel.

02

Supplier & Service Deductions

Final refund amount may be reduced by airline, hotel, transport, gateway, or other third-party cancellation charges.

03

Refund Processing Time

After approval, refunds are initiated within 7 to 10 business days. Bank settlement timelines may add extra days.

04

Non-Refundable Items

Visa fees, permit charges, convenience fees, and selected promotional or last-minute bookings may be non-refundable.

05

Rescheduling & Modifications

Date changes are subject to availability and revised pricing. Difference in fare or package rate must be paid by traveler.

06

Support & Status Updates

Our team shares status updates at each stage. Keep booking ID and registered contact details ready for faster resolution.

Refund Eligibility Based on Cancellation Time

General cancellation slabs for standard package bookings before travel date.

Time Before Travel Date Refund Amount
30 Days or More 100% Refund
15 - 29 Days 50% Refund
Less than 15 Days No Refund

Important Notes:

Refunds will be made to the original payment method only.

Processing timelines may vary on weekends, bank holidays, and partner closures.

Special fares or festive offers may follow separate cancellation terms mentioned at booking.
